	Gait is the movement pattern of limbs during walking over a solid surface. It varies based on speed, terrain, maneuvering or efficiency of energy. This movement is unique for each human and can be used for recognizing persons from afar, without the need of their cooperation or physical contact, whereas fingerprint, iris or facial do need the physical access or their cooperation \cite{biometrics-comparison}.
